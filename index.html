KJDB2021

Korea-Japan (Japan-Korea) Database Workshop 2021

03-04 December 2021, Zao, Yamagata, Japan

Greetings

The 16th Korea-Japan(Japan-Korea) Database Workshop (KJDB 2021) will be held from December 03 to 04, 2021 at Takamiya Hotel Lucent in Yamagata, Japan. This workshop is an annual joint workshop with honorable scholars in Korea and Japan (and possibly other countries) for sharing the scope and the latest activities in new areas of of database and database-related technologies. Participants will engage in interactive discussions of their activities and priorities for database technologies and database-related technologies. We hope that the output of this workshop will serve as a basis for a roadmap of opportunities and initiatives for the upcoming years.

Registration

Free of charge for all the participants.
Please contact us (kjdb2021 [at] gmail.com) if you are a KJDB member and would like to participate onsite.
